Who we are

A Trusted and Innovative Leader in Infratech, Real Estate, and Agro Farming

Yosh Group is a 17-year-old organization with extensive experience in the field of infratech solutions, providing innovative and cutting-edge services across diverse industries. Their successful real estate ventures in Palghar district and Mumbai have earned them a reputation as a reliable and trusted partner. Yosh Group also operates in the agro farming sector, delivering premium quality fruits and vegetables. With a commitment to excellence, sustainable solutions, and customer-centric approach, Yosh Group continues to be a leading player in the infratech, real estate, and agro farming sectors.
